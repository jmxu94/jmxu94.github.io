Pix!

Introduction

Hello! Welcome to the hobby page for a small pixel game that I'm working on! This is just a side project that I dabble on here and there for fun. Enjoy your stay. :)

About Pix

Pix is a pixel-art RPG game. The main character is a small silly rabbit that gets lost from their family and the story progresses through their adventures as they find their way home. They meet a sly fox, accidentally step in some poop, and other rambunctious situations.

Art

The artwork for Pix is made using Asesprite on Steam and animated into gifs using Adobe CS6.

While some artwork is on this welcome page, you can check out the art here: Artwork

Music

The music for Pix is made using Fl Studio 12 and Garageband.

Gameplay

The gameplay follows traditional jRPGs in that characters move following WASD or arrow keys. Action and menu inputs have not yet been considered. The game is made using GameMaker: Studio.
