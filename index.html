Greetings! My name is Julia Xu but you can call me Julie.

I am an upbeat developer and artist currently residing in the mysteriously sunny Seattle!

I majored in engineering at the University of Washington (Materials Engineering B.S.) and then pursued Dev Bootcamp, a web development coding bootcamp.

I've always had an avid interest in tech applications that aim to create happy and healthy individuals. Thusly, I sought out training so I could couple my technical interest in engineering with my passion for coding in order to have a direct hand in creating projects that generate excitement.

I aim to make applications come to life and seek to create happy communities.
