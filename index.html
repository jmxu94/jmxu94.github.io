About Pix

[scroll down]

Pix is a pixel-art adventure RPG game. You, the main character, play as a small rabbit that gets lost from their family. You wake up in a grassy field. Your goal is to find your way home.

The story progresses through meeting some other fellow woodland creatures (friend or foe), fighting by utilizing the resources around you, and figuring out how and why you got lost in the first place.

Art

The artwork for Pix is made using Asesprite on Steam and animated into gifs using Adobe CS6.

Music

The music for Pix is made using Fl Studio 12 on PC and Garageband on Mac.

Gameplay

The gameplay follows traditional jRPGs - characters move following WASD or arrow keys. Action and menu inputs are utilized via "X", "C", and the space bar keys. The game is made using GameMaker: Studio.
